What is Islamophobia?

Often times today we fall to the common misconception that in our age hate crimes aren't as much of a problem as they were in the past on the contrary, hate crimes have gotten worse and become more frequent as the years go by.
According to the latest statistics provided by the FBI in 2017 "In 2017, 16,149 law enforcement agencies participated in the Hate Crime Statistics Program. Of these agencies, 2,040 reported 7,175 hate crime incidents involving 8,437 offenses"

What exactly is the definition of Islamophobia?
Islamophobia is a fear towards Muslims, a result of the attack on 9/11 since 9/11 was lead by a terrorist group run by Muslims. After that attack fear spread across America and people feared that any Muslim could be the next big terrorist or cause the next big catastrophe

What are the characteristics of Islamophobia
Islamophobics typically compare Muslims to terrorist, Islamophobics live under the impression that all Muslims are or could be a terrorist. The result of this idea is often racism and discrimination directed towards Muslims.

Fighting Islamophobia

There's quite a bit you can do to contribute to fighting Islamophobia, here is a list of some things you can do to help combat these hate-driven acts

  1. Support groups like CAIR who do what they can to combat Islamophobia, no need to join, just support
  2. A lot of hate comes in from the media so combat the media by spreading positivity and combatting the hate online
  3. "If you see something say something" definitely applies to acts of hate, if you see someone getting attacked, be the intervention or drive the attacker away
  4. Become informed and inform others about the problem, it's easier to stop something when you understand what it is and its true nature

These are among many ways to put up a fight against Islamophobia, we can all contribute different amounts so let's all pitch in what we can so that we can help
